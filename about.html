Mission

“Our mission is to provide free tax preparation, tax education,
and financial asset building education to all qualifying Polk County Citizens.”

    Goals

  • •   Prepare free tax returns for Polk County families and individuals with incomes less than $57,000

  • •   Facilitate matching those that are unbanked with banking institutions

  • •   Promote saving by starting with a portion of the tax refund.

  • •   Provide information for financial education and asset building programs/resources.
